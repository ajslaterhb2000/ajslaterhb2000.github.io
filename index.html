About Me

Hello, I'm Andrew Slater, a computer science graduate from the University of California San Diego.

With a strong passion for software engineering, I have specialized in machine learning, graphics and rendering, algorithms, and networking throughout my academic journey. I have gained practical experience in these areas, along with 1+ years of industry experience.

During my time at UCSD, I excelled in courses such as Data Science, Data Structures and Algorithms, Deep Learning, Linear Algebra, Computer Graphics, Computer Rendering, Computer Animation, Networking, Computer Vision, Operating Systems, and Image Processing.

I am a TensorFlow Certified Developer, demonstrating my proficiency in building and training neural network models using TensorFlow 2.x. I have successfully worked on image classification tasks, natural language processing (NLP), and time series analysis using deep learning techniques.

During my internship at Boeing, I developed and debugged Python scripts to enhance the efficiency of mechanical engineers' workflow. I am adept at Python programming and possess valuable soft skills such as time management, communication, and logical thinking.

In my recent project at TagGuys UCSD, I contributed to the development of a 4-player networked video game with an art style inspired by Fall Guys. This project involved utilizing C++, OpenGL, Boost, Asio, and SFML. I gained experience in agile development, pair programming, debugging, and teamwork.

Additionally, I worked on the Quaternion Interpolation project, demonstrating the smooth transitions between poses using quaternion representation. I implemented quaternion interpolation techniques such as SLERP and Catmull-Rom algorithm using C++ and OpenGL.

In a personal project, I created a raytracer that handles sphere intersections and mirror and diffuse surfaces. This project was inspired by Andrew Kelsers' raytracer business card and showcased my knowledge from the rendering course.

I am proficient in programming languages such as Python (including Pandas, PyTorch, TensorFlow, NumPy, Scikit-learn), C/C++ (OpenGL, CUDA), HTML/CSS, JavaScript, and Java. I am experienced with tools like Linux, Git, and have proficiency in various software applications.

With strong soft skills in time management, teamwork, problem-solving, and documentation, I am dedicated to delivering high-quality results in an agile environment.

If you have any exciting opportunities or would like to connect, please feel free to reach out to me through the contact form below. I'm excited to bring my skills and passion to contribute to your team.
